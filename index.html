Who We Are

YOUR TRUSTED PROCUREMENT PARTNER

Futaba Decoration was established with a clear purpose: to simplify how construction professionals source and procure building materials. We operate on both sides of the supply chain — acting as a trusted procurement partner to developers, contractors, and sub-contractors, and as a direct customer to factories and manufacturers worldwide.

With over two decades of industry experience, we have cultivated an extensive network of verified suppliers, manufacturers, and logistics partners across the GCC and beyond. From structural systems and formwork to premium interior finishes, bespoke joinery, and cabinetry — we manage the complete supply process so our clients can focus on what matters most: delivering outstanding projects.

What We Offer

COMPREHENSIVE SUPPLY SOLUTIONS

Materials Supply

A comprehensive range of building and interior materials — kitchens, cabinetry, wardrobes, decking systems, facades, timber, wall mounts, formwork, scaffolding, joinery, and storage solutions — sourced and supplied to your exact project requirements.

Procurement Management

We manage procurement across the full supply chain — acting as a supply partner to developers, contractors, and sub-contractors, and as a direct purchasing channel to factories and manufacturers. From supplier identification and price negotiation to order management and documentation, we handle the entire process.

Interior & Fit-Out Sourcing

Specialist sourcing for interior and fit-out materials — including tiles, flooring, wall finishes, ceiling systems, decorative panels, sanitary ware, and bespoke finishing elements. We procure directly from manufacturers to ensure quality standards and competitive pricing at every stage.

Logistics, Delivery & Port Management

End-to-end freight management from factory to project location — including port clearance, customs coordination, inland logistics, warehousing, and last-mile site delivery. We manage the full freight chain so materials arrive on schedule and in perfect condition.

Project Estimation Support

We help developers and contractors produce accurate material takeoffs and procurement cost estimates at the planning stage — providing a reliable budget foundation before any orders are placed, and helping avoid costly surprises mid-project.

Bulk Supply & Long-Term Contracts

Structured supply agreements and bulk procurement contracts for developers and contractors managing multiple sites or ongoing programmes. Consistent material availability, volume pricing, and priority fulfilment — built for scale and long-term partnership.
